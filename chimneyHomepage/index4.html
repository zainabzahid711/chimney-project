Hardware Flexibility

Use your existing sever and allocate your storage and bandwidth for 0Chain DCS
(Decentralized Cloud Storage). Or you can rent a server and you'll be a part of
making data more private, secure, giving ownership of data back to the people.

Easy Install & Setup

Get started with either an express or CLI setup. Small pieces of encrypted data from
0chainDCS will be stored at random on your server. Once your server is set up, all you
need to do is make sure your setup is online and running properly—we'll do the rest.

Get Paid

After your server is setup and verified, you'll be compensated for the storage and
bandwidth you provide for 0Chain users. Instead of operating a costly data center,
we pay you for the resources used by users.
